Random Codepoint Selector

If you are looking to use a new codepoint for a protocol, look no further.

This understands the registries from Section 22 of QUIC, Section 11 of HTTP/3, and Section 5.4 of HTTP Datagrams and the Capsule Protocol.

The tool is deterministic and checks for codepoints that are either reserved or already allocated.
